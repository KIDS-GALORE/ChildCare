Welcome To Our Childcare
It's Nice To Meet You
Tell Me More

Welcome to KIDS GALORE, your trusted partner in providing exceptional care and nurturing for your child. We understand that choosing the right childcare facility for your little one is one of the most important decisions you'll make as a parent. That's why we're here to make that decision easier for you.

Our Mission

At KIDS GALORE, our mission is to create a safe, stimulating, and loving environment where your child can learn, grow, and flourish. We believe that each child is a unique individual, and our dedicated team is committed to providing the personalized attention and support they need to thrive.

Our Philosophy

We believe that early childhood is a critical period for development, and we take this responsibility seriously. Our philosophy revolves around providing a well-rounded educational experience that fosters creativity, curiosity, and social development. Through a balance of structured activities and free play, we help children build a strong foundation for future learning.

Our Promise

At KIDS GALORE, we promise to provide a loving and enriching environment where your child can grow, learn, and develop to their fullest potential. Your child's well-being is our top priority, and we're committed to fostering a love for learning that will last a lifetime.

Benefits for You

  • SAFE ENVIROMENT

    Offering care from one to five days a week, extra hours, and no late fees.

  • Adaptive Learning

    Daily choice of activities, Painting, Playdough, Fine Motor and Gross Motor activities, Dramatic play, Arts & Crafts, Baking, Science, Field Trips & more.

  • Flexible, Excellent Child Care

    Flexibility is a top priority for Kids Galore, which provides care one to five days a week, long hours, and no late fees.

  • Qualified teachers

    Kids Galore has dedicated, professional and caring teachers who provide a warm, loving and stable environment for all the children.
